The Future of Our Chapter




Our TSA Chapter is only about a year and a half old. Although we are currently quite small, we believe the Central Magnet chapter has a bright future.

At this year's state conference, we hope to add to our list of finalists and awards. With the experience we gained at last year's conference, we feel more prepared. Our chapter is also starting to grow and become more established; this year's projects have been better prepared and we have even gained members that are not students of the engineering program.


By the end of this school year, we'd like to have offical officer elections. Having students offically in officer positions would increase our stability and organization for next year, giving us a better framework as we continue to grow.

Looking further into the future, we hope to attend nationals as a school in the next few years.
