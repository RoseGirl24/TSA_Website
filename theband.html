Cardboard Fantasy is the hit band that is currently taking the world by storm. The game-changing group first captured the hearts of fans with an incredible live performance, and soon drew the attention of critics and the media. This warm welcome to the music scene has prompted Cardboard Fantasy to plan their first offical tour, "It's All About The Cardboard," which sold out almost immediately and has since had new venues added.

The band got its start at a TASK party. Although it was Rash that drew the task that originally inspired the group, Cardboard Fantasy insists that no one member can take credit for starting up the band. The cardboard called to them, and they've been jamming together ever since.
